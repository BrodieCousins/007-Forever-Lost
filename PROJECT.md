Project Outline
Goal: To eliminate Jane Sever, a billionaire philanthropist planning a global nuclear take-over.
Context: You are Bond, James Bond. MI6 has dropped you off near Jane Sever’s main facility, located in Jakar, Bhutan. Sever is a supplier of nuclear-powered weapons, believed to be scheming a plan to utilise these weapons for her own benefit, to gain control over the world.

Game Mechanics
Security Cameras
Bond is unable to enter rooms with security cameras and escape the facility alive. The player must remove the guard located in the security room, before he is able to venture further throughout the map. Even with the scientist disguise the cameras recognise bond as an intruder.
Death Message
You step into <x> room. Before you can blink, the lights around you flicker to red. An obnoxious blaring siren sounds throughout the entire facility. The doors around you close and lock, reinforced metal doors slamming shut right in front of your face. Your fate is sealed. You draw your pistol to start firing at the walls, the door, anything to find some sort of weakness. But there isn’t one. Desperation consumes you, banging on the walls. As time passes you find yourself too exhausted to continue, gas fills the room. James Bond… is dead.
